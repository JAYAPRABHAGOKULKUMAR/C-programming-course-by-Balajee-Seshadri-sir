## Problem Statement
Write a loop program to print the sum of numbers from 6 to 1.

## Explanation
The sum of numbers 6 + 5 + 4 + 3 + 2 + 1 is 21.

## Approach
1. Initialize `i` to 6 and `sum` to 0.
2. Add `i` to `sum`.
3. Decrement `i` until it reaches 1.
4. Print the sum.
